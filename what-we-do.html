What We Do

Conservation

Conservation is at the forefront of the BWC’s mantra, we started this organisation with the plan to help conserve the habitats of the most endangered species. We have already made changes in all of the UK’s major parks and several National Trust sites. We have planted over 200 acres of trees all with the aim to increase the amount of natural land within the United Kingdom.

Knowledge

At BWC we believe that teaching someone to fish is a better alternative than giving them one. We use our weekly newsletter to help teach people ways in which they can improve habitats in their local area, or simply connect more with nature and what is around them.

In our newsletter we offer free tips and tricky to improve our outdoor space, ways in which we can improve communal areas, what we’ve previously accomplished and many more. Why not sign up.
